## We're changing,   but why now?

### Every superhero has a backstory…

Chennai: A hot summer day, a flat tyre, and a long chat. That was the lethal concoction that killed our jobs and mothered TING!

Two offices: one in Chennai and the other in Mumbai. Our laptops, our friendship, and the faith we had built at MICA became the foundation of our passion, our work, our agency: TING.

A few years ago, it was just the three of us, cleaning our office, making our chai, and hoping the phone would ring!

And then, years later, three became a family of 500+. Three freshers straight outta college became CEOs, COOs, and whatnot. Blue turned yellow. A tiny 200 sq. ft. room grew into large offices. Two cities expanded into nine. Local went global. And the Ting tortoise (yeah, we’re not so much the hasty hare, or maybe we are!) kept ambling along, one step at a time.

So ting became our thing. Our ting!

### solutions

With our specialised verticals, the idea was to go deep and build expertise within each solution. While we’re all for Jack and all his trades, we believe there’s something different about being the master. Through relentless focus and by marrying the left brain with the right,

our journey from being the Jack of all trades to the Rajni or SRK of this industry has begun. We’re not saying we’re there yet, but we’re hoping you’ll agree we’re on the way. Little pompous, aren’t we?
